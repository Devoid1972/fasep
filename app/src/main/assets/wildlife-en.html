Wild boar :

Wild boar can reach 3 to 6.5 feet in length, 21.6 to 39.3 inches in height and 90 to 700 pounds in weight. It has long, rubbery snout that is used for digging of underground roots and bulbs. It feeds mainly on the seed, fruits, leaves, berries, eggs, mice, lizards, worms and snakes. Wild boars are nocturnal animals. They will spend 12h in sleep during the day, hidden in the nests made of leaves.
